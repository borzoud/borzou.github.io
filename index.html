Career

I started my career as a Chemical Engineer in the university of Sharif (Iran’s best university) in which thanks to its noted professors I gained a lot of hands-on experience in both process design and control. Some of the topics covered are: thermodynamics, instrumentation and process control, analytical and physical chemistry, genetics project course and bio-engineering, project management and so on (Click for details). Plus, I spent my internship on an oil rig in Ahwaz, South of Iran, which facilitated my studies on “chemical characteristics and production methods of Xanthan gum” for my thesis. Additionally, I performed number of process simulations using HYSIS for oil industry.

After graduation I started to work in Azaran as a process engineer for iron pelletizing of largest open-pit mine in the region (5 MTPA). During three years of working as a process engineer, I honed my skills in : process design and control, equipment sizing, Siemens control system, P&ID development and other documentations like PFD and Process specifications. I had also led number of test activities to validate the design and specify parameters for parameter designs. During those years, I learned a lot about safety procedures, valves and instruments and made lots of contacts with vendors and suppliers all over the world.

Motivated by inspiration of creating sustainable human societies, I studies MSc of Sustainable Energy Engineering at KTH in which I’ve performed numerous projects alone and in a group. For my thesis I studied throttling effect on axial compressor blades through CFD simulations and experimental validations, where I used ANSYS. What my studies gave me was a rigid understanding of the energy technologies and infrastructures which became very handy later.

Working in Cortus as a Process Automation Engineer , was a big shift in my career. In those 5 years, I was involved in wide range of activities, namely; Project Coordination, Process Control and Design, Electrical & Automation Design, Control System Selection, FAT, SAT, R&D, Optimization & Data Analysis. An example of one my experiences is once we realized particles in our pyrolysis gas are settling in the pipes. I suggested to use multicyclones which I did the simulation for and selected the instrumentations. After project commissioning through analysis of sensor data and collaboration with Mechanical department, we managed to improve the efficiency significantly. Some of the activities I have done are listed below:

  • Produced high level function design process specifications (P&ID, FDS, Loop Diagrams, etc.).
  • Automated the whole operation in an unprecedented level(DCS programming), resulted in a significantly lower requirement for operators and safer operations.
  • Built a data server using MSSQL for applications meta data.
  • Overseeing energy operations and coordinating with key partners. Built a strong communication with industry specific suppliers.
  • FAT & SAT, HAZID studies, HAZOP, FMEA, Cause and effect and risk graph analysis
  • Valve & instrument selection, pipe sizing and other engineering calculations

Services

Control system design

Whether you need DCS, SCADA or a PLC for your process, with more than 5 years of experience, I can design a proper system according to your need. I have prior experience of working with Siemens, ABB, Allen Bradely and Yokagawa systems. Range of service includes:

  • Contacting with suppliers, quoting and other services
  • Documentation packages such as FDS, UML, Loop diagrams, etc.
  • Programming controllers and PLC
  • Hardware selection
  • Setup of communication protocols such as OPC-UA, Pub/Sub, etc.

Valves, instrumentaion and control

Having worked in process wise demanding industries (renewable power generation, mining, oil & gas, water treatment), I can assist you with the design of your control loops. This includes selection of the valves from body to solenoid to electronics, selection of most suitable instrumentations in accord with the process conditions and designing advanced controllers.
Throughout my career, not only have I have been in contact with a wide range of vendors, but also due to my experiences in commissioning, operation and design phase, I can provide robust solutions which would excel in most challenging applications.
Additionally, I have experience in simulating control loops in HYSIS, SIMULINK and other process simulation software.

Process engineering and safety

With a profound background in science, I can understand, design and simulate most challenging of the process conditions to come up with standard practical solutions. To do so I can perform following tasks:

  • P&ID, PFD, Technical Specifications and other engineering calculations.
  • Process simulations to calculate mass & energy balance, cost estimation, pipe sizing, optimization and more.
  • Supervision and coordination of teams to overcome process obstacles.
  • Coordination of FAT, SAT and comissioning tests.

On the other hand, during my career I was actively involved in process safety; namely: HAZID, HAZOP, Loss prevention, Critical control points CCPs, CAPA and FMEA. My range of responsibilities included:

  • Managed a team of engineers in the design and implementation of a new safety management system.
  • Coordinated with cross-functional teams to develop and implement corrective and preventive actions to address process safety hazards.

Data Analyze & Optimization
